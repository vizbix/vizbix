About Vizbix

Vizbix is an intelligence engine for founders. We build free calculators, tracking templates, and AI-powered software to help you spot profit leaks and grow smarter.

Why Vizbix exists

Most business owners track top-line sales but struggle to quickly answer the questions that actually matter: “Am I actually making a profit?”, “Which products are dragging me down?”, or “How much should I charge?”

We started by building simple, free calculators to answer those questions. Today, Vizbix has evolved into a complete intelligence suite. Whether you just need to do quick markup math, or you want our AI Consultant to analyze your entire product catalog, we are here to turn your numbers into clear, confident decisions.
