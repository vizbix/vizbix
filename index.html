About us

A large part of running a business comes down to calculable decisions: pricing a product, planning expenses, checking profit margins, and understanding when you’ll break even. Many owners don’t calculate these things because it takes time, feels confusing, or the right tools aren’t available. And then decisions are made on guesswork instead of clarity.

We build tools that make every business calculation easy for anyone to use.
