About Vizbix

Vizbix is a small collection of practical business calculators built to make everyday decisions easier — pricing, profit, costs, and growth planning.

Why Vizbix exists

Most business owners track sales, but struggle to quickly answer simple questions like: “Am I actually making profit?”, “How much should I charge?”, or “How many sales do I need to break even?”

Vizbix is built to remove that confusion. The tools are simple, fast, and designed for real business use — not finance textbooks.

What you’ll find here

Simple calculators

Profit margin, break-even, ROI, markup, discount, GST, targets, and cash runway.

Clear explanations

Every tool includes a quick explanation so you understand the number, not just see it.

Business-first design

Made for founders and small teams. No jargon. No complex spreadsheets.

Fast and lightweight

Works on mobile and desktop. No login required.

How to use Vizbix

  • Pick a tool from the homepage.
  • Enter your numbers (revenue, cost, price, etc.).
  • Use the result to make a better decision — pricing, budget, targets, or planning.

Important note

Vizbix tools provide calculations based on the numbers you enter. They are meant for guidance and planning, not legal, tax, or financial advice. For critical decisions, always verify with your accountant or advisor.
