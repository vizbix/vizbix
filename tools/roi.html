How ROI is calculated

  • Profit = Total Return Value − Investment Cost
  • ROI (%) = (Profit ÷ Investment Cost) × 100

💡 Tip: For marketing ROI (ROAS), make sure your "Investment Cost" includes ad spend, and your "Return Value" is your gross margin, not just total revenue.
