How runway is calculated

  • Monthly Burn = Expenses − Revenue (if provided)
  • Runway (months) = Cash Balance ÷ Monthly Burn
  • Runway (days) ≈ Runway months × 30

Tip: If your runway is short, reduce expenses, improve collections, or increase revenue. Always keep buffer for unexpected costs.
