How GST is calculated

  • Add GST: Final = Base × (1 + GST% ÷ 100)
  • Remove GST: Base = Inclusive ÷ (1 + GST% ÷ 100)
  • GST Amount = Final − Base

Tip: For invoices, always confirm whether your pricing is GST-inclusive or exclusive.
