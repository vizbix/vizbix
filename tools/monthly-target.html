How it is calculated

  • Daily Target = Monthly Target ÷ Working Days
  • Weekly Target = Monthly Target ÷ Weeks in Month (Average is 4.33)
  • Orders/Day = Daily Target ÷ Average Order Value (AOV)

💡 Tip: If your daily order target feels impossible to hit, you must either increase your prices (AOV) or create high-value bundles.
